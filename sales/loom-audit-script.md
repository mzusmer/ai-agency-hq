# Loom Audit Script - Summit Automation

A personalized Loom video is one of the highest-converting outreach tools available. This script walks through exactly what to say and show on screen for any local service business.

---

## What Is a Loom Audit?

A Loom audit is a 2 to 4 minute personalized screen recording you send to a specific business. You show their Google Business Profile, call out a missed call problem specific to them, and give a quick preview of what the solution looks like.

It feels personal. It shows you did your homework. And it makes it easy for the owner to say yes to a call.

---

## Preparation (Do This Before Recording)

1. Look up the business on Google Maps
2. Note their name, star rating, number of reviews, and whether they have a "Call" button active
3. Check their website (or note if they don't have one)
4. Note their listed hours (this tells you when they are likely missing after-hours calls)
5. Open their Google Business Profile in a browser tab
6. Have a second tab open with a brief demo of the automation flow (GHL demo account or a short clip)

---

## What to Show on Screen

- Start on their Google Business Profile or website
- Zoom in on their phone number and call button
- Reference their review count and activity
- Transition to showing the automation flow (the sample SMS conversation)

---

## Script

> **[0:00 to 0:20 - Introduction]**
>
> "Hey [First Name], this is [Your Name] from Summit Automation. I put together this quick video specifically for [Business Name]. It will take about two minutes, and I think you will find it useful."

---

> **[0:20 to 1:00 - Show Their Profile and the Problem]**
>
> "So here is [Business Name] on Google. You've got [X] reviews, which is great. That tells me people are finding you and calling you.
>
> Here's the thing: I tested what happens when someone calls your number and doesn't get an answer. I called at [time] on [day] and it went straight to voicemail.
>
> For a customer who needs [type of service] right now, that is often enough for them to just hit the back button and call the next business on the list. And based on the call volume a business with your reviews typically gets, there is a real chance you are losing leads you already worked to generate."

---

> **[1:00 to 2:00 - Show the Solution]**
>
> "Here's what we do. The moment that call comes in and nobody answers, our system fires off a text within seconds. Something like this: [show the first SMS on screen].
>
> The customer replies, and the AI takes over. It asks what they need, gets their address or location, finds out how urgent it is, and asks when they want to be contacted. [Show a short example conversation if you have one.]
>
> By the time [Business Name] gets the notification, you already know the customer's name, what they need, and when they want to hear back. Your team follows up on a warm lead instead of a cold callback."

---

> **[2:00 to 2:30 - Call to Action]**
>
> "I would love to put together a full free missed call audit for [Business Name] specifically and show you what this would look like for your setup.
>
> It is free, takes about 15 minutes on a call, and you will walk away knowing exactly how many calls you might be losing and what that is worth in revenue.
>
> Reply to this video or text me at [number] and we will get something on the calendar. Thanks, [First Name], hope this was useful."

---

## Tips for Recording

- Record in one take when possible. Authenticity beats polish.
- Keep it under 3 minutes. After 3 minutes, drop-off increases significantly.
- Use Loom's click-highlight feature to draw attention to what you are showing.
- Keep your face in the corner the whole time. It builds trust.
- Do not read from a script word for word. Use this as a guide and talk naturally.
- Re-record if you stumble badly, but minor stumbles are fine and human.

## Customizing by Industry

Mention the type of business and the specific pain point in the video:

| Industry | Pain Point to Mention |
|---|---|
| HVAC | "Customers calling during a job or after hours when no one picks up" |
| Med Spa | "Appointment requests going to voicemail and booking elsewhere" |
| Dental | "New patient inquiries not getting followed up fast enough" |
| Plumbing | "Emergency calls that go unanswered and go to a competitor" |
| Landscaping | "Quote requests coming in during jobs with no one to answer" |

## Where to Send Loom Videos

- Email: paste the Loom link in the body (subject line: "Quick video I put together for [Company Name]")
- Instagram DM: paste the link after the first exchange
- Text message: if you have their cell, a plain text with the link works well
