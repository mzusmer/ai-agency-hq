# Follow-Up Sequence - Summit Automation

This is the multi-touch follow-up plan for local service business prospects who have shown interest but have not yet committed to a demo or signed on.

---

## When to Use This Sequence

Use this after any of the following:
- You sent a cold email and got no reply
- You spoke briefly on the phone and they said "send me more info"
- You sent a Loom video and they watched it but did not reply
- You had a demo and they said "I need to think about it"
- You sent a proposal and heard nothing back

---

## The Sequence

### Touch 1 - Initial Outreach
Send the first email, DM, or make the first call (covered in `cold-email-scripts.md`, `instagram-dm-scripts.md`, and `cold-call-script.md`).

---

### Touch 2 - Day 3 (Follow-Up #1)

**Email:**
> Subject: Re: Quick question about missed calls
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Just checking back in on the note I sent a few days ago. No pressure at all.
>
> I can put together a free missed call audit for [Business Name] showing how many leads you are likely losing and what that could mean in revenue. Takes about 10 minutes for me to put together.
>
> Want me to send it over?
>
> [Your Name]

---

### Touch 3 - Day 7 (Loom Video or Specific Value Add)

Send a personalized Loom video (see `loom-audit-script.md`) or, if you already sent one, follow up on it.

**Email:**
> Subject: Quick video I put together for [Business Name]
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Recorded this short video specifically for you. Takes about 2 minutes.
>
> [Loom link]
>
> Shows you what we found looking at [Business Name]'s missed call situation and what it would look like to fix it. Happy to walk through it on a call if the video raises questions.
>
> [Your Name]

---

### Touch 4 - Day 14 (Phone Call)

Call them directly. Use the cold call script. Leave a voicemail only on the first attempt.

**Voicemail:**
> "Hey [First Name], this is [Your Name] from Summit Automation. I have sent a couple of notes over the past two weeks. Just wanted to put a voice to the name. Give me a call at [number] or reply to any of my emails when you get a chance. Thanks."

---

### Touch 5 - Day 21 (Break-Up Email)

This is the last contact before moving on.

**Email:**
> Subject: Closing the loop on this
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> I have reached out a few times and haven't heard back, so I am guessing the timing isn't right.
>
> No hard feelings at all. Running a local business is a full-time job and a half.
>
> If you ever want to look at automating your missed call follow-up, I am here. Just reach out whenever.
>
> Good luck.
>
> [Your Name]

---

## Follow-Up After a Demo (Prospect Said "I Need to Think About It")

### Same Day as Demo
Send a brief thank-you with a summary of what was discussed and the proposal link.

> "Hey [First Name], great talking today. Here's the proposal: [link]. Let me know if anything needs clarifying. Happy to jump on a quick call if questions come up."

### Day 3
> "Hey [First Name], just checking in. Did you get a chance to look at the proposal? Any questions I can clear up for you?"

### Day 7
> "Hi [First Name], following up one more time on the proposal. I have a couple of setup slots opening up next week and wanted to check if [Business Name] is still a fit. Happy to hop on a 10-minute call to answer anything."

### Day 14 (Final)
> "Hi [First Name], I don't want to keep following up if the timing isn't right. If you're still interested, let me know and we can pick up where we left off. If not, no worries."

---

## After Closing (Client Signed)

Send a welcome email within 24 hours:

> "Hi [First Name], welcome to Summit Automation! Really glad to have [Business Name] on board.
>
> Here's what happens next:
>
> 1. I will send the onboarding questionnaire today (takes about 5 minutes)
> 2. We will schedule your onboarding call for this week
> 3. Build and testing take 5 to 7 business days
> 4. Then you are live
>
> If anything comes up before then, just text or email me directly. Talk soon!"

---

## Tracking

Log every follow-up in `data/lead-list.md` under Outreach Status and Last Contacted columns. Set a reminder for each next follow-up date.
