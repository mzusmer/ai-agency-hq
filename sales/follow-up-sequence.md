# Follow-Up Sequence - Summit Automation

This is the multi-touch follow-up plan for roofing company prospects who have shown interest but have not yet committed to a demo or signed on.

---

## When to Use This Sequence

Use this sequence after any of the following:

- You sent a cold email and got no reply
- You spoke briefly on the phone and they said "send me more info"
- You sent a Loom video and they watched it but did not reply
- You had a demo and they said "I need to think about it"
- You sent a proposal and heard nothing back

---

## The Sequence

### Touch 1 - Initial Outreach
Send the first email, DM, or make the first call (covered in `cold-email-scripts.md`, `instagram-dm-scripts.md`, and `cold-call-script.md`).

---

### Touch 2 - Day 3 (Follow-Up #1)

**Email:**
> Subject: Re: Quick question about missed calls
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Just checking back in on the note I sent a few days ago. No pressure at all.
>
> I can put together a free missed call audit for [Business Name] showing how many leads you're likely losing and what it's worth in revenue. Takes about 10 minutes for me to pull together.
>
> Want me to send it over?
>
> [Your Name]

---

### Touch 3 - Day 7 (Loom Video or Specific Value Add)

Send a personalized Loom video (see `loom-audit-script.md`) OR if you already sent one, follow up on it.

**Email:**
> Subject: Quick video I put together for [Business Name]
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Recorded this short video for you specifically. It takes about 2 minutes.
>
> [Loom link]
>
> Shows you what we found looking at [Business Name]'s missed call situation and what it would look like to fix it. Happy to walk through it on a call if the video raises questions.
>
> [Your Name]

---

### Touch 4 - Day 14 (Phone Call)

Call them directly. Use the cold call script. Leave a voicemail only on the first attempt.

**Voicemail:**
> "Hey [First Name], this is [Your Name] from Summit Automation. I've sent a couple of notes over the past two weeks. I know things are busy. Just wanted to put a voice to the name. Give me a call at [number] or reply to any of my emails when you get a chance. Thanks."

---

### Touch 5 - Day 21 (Break-Up Email)

This is the last contact before you move on.

**Email:**
> Subject: Closing the loop on this
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back, so I'm guessing the timing isn't right.
>
> No hard feelings at all. Running a roofing company is a full-time job and a half.
>
> If you ever want to look at automating your missed call follow-up, I'm here. Just reach out whenever.
>
> Good luck this season.
>
> [Your Name]

---

## Follow-Up After a Demo (Prospect Said "I Need to Think About It")

### Day 1 - Same Day as Demo
Send a brief thank-you with a summary of what was discussed and the proposal link.

> "Hey [First Name], great talking today. Here's the proposal we went over: [link]. Let me know if anything needs clarifying. Happy to hop on a quick call if questions come up."

### Day 3 - Nudge
> "Hey [First Name], just checking in. Did you get a chance to look at the proposal? Any questions I can clear up for you?"

### Day 7 - Light Pressure
> "Hi [First Name], want to follow up one more time on the proposal. I have a few setup slots opening up next week and wanted to check if [Business Name] is still a fit. Happy to hop on a 10-minute call to answer anything."

### Day 14 - Final
> "Hi [First Name], I don't want to keep following up if the timing isn't right. If you're still interested, let me know and we can pick up where we left off. If not, no worries at all."

---

## After Closing (Client Signed)

Send a welcome email within 24 hours:

> "Hi [First Name], welcome to Summit Automation! Really glad to have [Business Name] on board.
>
> Here's what happens next:
>
> 1. I'll send over the onboarding questionnaire today (takes about 5 minutes)
> 2. We'll schedule your onboarding call for sometime this week
> 3. Build and testing will take 5 to 7 business days
> 4. Then you'll be live
>
> If anything comes up before then, just text or email me directly. Talk soon!"

---

## Tracking

Log every follow-up in `data/roofing-lead-list.md` under Outreach Status and Last Contacted columns. Set a reminder for each next follow-up date.
