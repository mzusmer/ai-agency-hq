# Instagram DM Scripts - Summit Automation

---

## Strategy

Instagram works well for outreach to owner-operated local service businesses. Many business owners are active on Instagram, post work photos and results, and respond to DMs faster than email.

Target accounts:
- Local service businesses with an active Instagram presence
- Posts showing work, before/afters, team photos, or behind-the-scenes
- 200 to 10,000 followers (active but not corporate)
- Based in your target markets

**Before messaging:** Follow the account and engage with 1 to 2 posts (a like or a genuine comment) before sending a DM. This significantly increases the chance they see and respond.

---

## DM 1 - First Touch (After Engaging With Their Content)

> Hey [First Name], love what you are doing with [Business Name]. The work you post looks great.

> Quick question: when a customer calls and you guys miss it (happens to everyone when you're busy with a job), do you have anything set up to follow up with them automatically?

> We help local [service type] businesses text missed callers instantly and qualify the lead while you're working. Happy to show you how it works if you're curious.

---

## DM 2 - If No Reply After 5 to 7 Days

> Hey [First Name], just wanted to follow up on the message I sent last week.

> We just helped a [service type] business in [State] recover several leads a month they were losing from missed calls. Happy to put together a free audit showing what that might look like for [Business Name].

> No pressure. Just thought it could be useful.

---

## DM 3 - If They Say "Tell Me More"

> Appreciate it! Here is the quick version:

> When someone calls your number and you don't answer, they usually move on to the next option. We fix that by texting them back within seconds. An AI handles the conversation, finds out what they need, and when they want to be contacted.

> You get a notification with all the details. By the time you follow up, they are already warmed up and expecting to hear from you.

> Setup takes about a week. One-time setup fee of $1,000, then $500 a month.

> Want to hop on a quick call or should I send you a short video showing how it works?

## DM - Offer the Free Audit

> Hey [First Name], I can put together a free audit for [Business Name] showing how many calls you might be losing and what that could mean in revenue.

> Takes me about 10 minutes. Want me to send it over?

---

## DM - After Sending a Loom Video

> Hey [First Name], sent over a short audit video for [Business Name]. Should be in your DMs or email if you have one linked.

> Totally free. Just want to show you what we found and see if it is useful. Let me know what you think!

---

## Response Handling

| They Say | You Reply |
|---|---|
| "Not interested" | "No worries at all! Good luck with the business." |
| "How much?" | Share pricing clearly. Do not apologize for it. |
| "Send me more info" | Send the one-pager or a Loom video |
| "Too busy right now" | "Totally get it. Mind if I follow up in [X weeks]?" |
| "We have someone who answers calls" | "Great. What about after-hours or weekends? That is usually where the gaps are." |
| No reply | Follow up once after 5 to 7 days, then move on |

---

## Industry-Specific Openers

Adapt the first DM based on the type of business:

| Industry | What to Comment On Before DMing |
|---|---|
| HVAC | A before/after of a clean installation or a technician out on a job |
| Landscaping | A yard transformation or a crew working photo |
| Med Spa | A treatment result photo or a "team spotlight" post |
| Cleaning | A satisfying before/after cleaning photo |
| Auto Detailing | A car transformation or a product showcase |
| Dental | A patient education post or a team photo |

---

## Notes

- Never DM more than 20 new accounts per day (risk of Instagram flagging for spam)
- Keep the first DM under 5 sentences
- Do not include links in the first DM (looks spammy)
- Genuine engagement before messaging makes a real difference in reply rates
- Track all outreach in `data/lead-list.md`
