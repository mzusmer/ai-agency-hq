# Instagram DM Scripts - Summit Automation

---

## Strategy

Instagram works well for outreach to owner-operated roofing companies. Many roofing business owners are active on Instagram, post job photos, and respond to DMs faster than email.

Target accounts:
- Roofing companies with an active Instagram presence
- Posts showing job sites, crews, before/afters
- 200 to 10,000 followers (too small means no activity, too big means corporate)
- Based in storm-prone states

**Important:** Follow the account first and engage with 1 to 2 posts (a like or a genuine comment) before sending a DM. This increases the chance they see and respond to your message.

---

## DM 1 - First Touch (After Engaging With Their Content)

> Hey [First Name], really like what you're doing with [Business Name]. The before/afters you post look great.

> Quick question: when a homeowner calls and you guys miss it (happens to everyone on a busy job), do you have anything set up to follow up with them automatically?

> We help roofing companies text back missed callers instantly and qualify the lead while you're on-site. Happy to show you how it works if you're curious.

---

## DM 2 - If No Reply After 5 to 7 Days

> Hey [First Name], just wanted to follow up on the message I sent last week.

> We just helped a roofing company in [State] recover several leads they were losing every month from missed calls. Happy to put together a free audit showing what that might look like for [Business Name] if you'd want to see it.

> No pressure at all. Just thought it could be useful.

---

## DM 3 - If They Say "Tell Me More"

> Appreciate it! Here's the quick version:

> When someone calls your number and you don't answer, they usually call the next roofer. We fix that by texting them back within seconds. An AI handles the conversation, collects their address, what's going on with the roof, and whether there's an insurance claim involved.

> You get a notification with all the details. By the time you call them back, they're warmed up and already expecting you.

> Setup takes about a week. One-time setup fee of $1,000, then $500/month.

> Want to hop on a quick call or should I send you a short video?

---

## DM - Offer the Free Audit

> Hey [First Name], I can do a quick free audit for [Business Name] showing how many leads you might be losing from missed calls and what that's worth in revenue.

> Takes me about 10 minutes to put together. Want me to send it over?

---

## DM - After Sending a Loom Video

> Hey [First Name], sent over a quick audit video for [Business Name]. Should be in your Instagram requests or email if you have one linked.

> Totally free. Just want to show you what we found and see if it's useful. Let me know what you think!

---

## Response Handling

| They Say | You Reply |
|---|---|
| "Not interested" | "No worries at all! Good luck with the season." |
| "How much?" | Share the pricing. Don't apologize for it. |
| "Send me more info" | Send the one-pager or a Loom video |
| "Too busy right now" | "Totally get it. Mind if I follow up in [X weeks]?" |
| "Do you work with commercial roofers?" | "We focus on residential right now. If that ever changes for you, let me know!" |
| No reply | Follow up once after 5 to 7 days. Then move on. |

---

## Notes

- Never DM more than 20 new accounts per day (risk of Instagram flagging)
- Keep the first DM short. Under 5 sentences.
- Don't include links in the first DM (looks spammy, gets filtered)
- Genuine engagement before messaging makes a big difference in reply rates
- Track outreach in the lead list (see `data/roofing-lead-list.md`)
