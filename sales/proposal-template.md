## What We Will Build

### 1. Missed Call Text-Back Automation

A GoHighLevel workflow that triggers instantly when your business number receives a missed call. The homeowner receives a text within seconds.

**What the homeowner receives:**
> "Hey, this is [Business Name]! Sorry we missed your call. Are you reaching out about a roofing issue? Reply YES and we'll get right back to you. Reply STOP to opt out."

### 2. AI Lead Qualification via SMS

An AI agent that handles the entire qualification conversation via text. It will collect:

- Homeowner's full name
- Property address
- Description of the roofing issue
- Whether they need a repair or full replacement
- Urgency level (active damage vs. general project)
- Insurance claim involvement
- Preferred appointment time

### 3. CRM Pipeline in GoHighLevel

A clean pipeline setup so every lead is tracked from first contact to closed job:

- New Lead
- No Response
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Estimate Scheduled
- Proposal Sent
- Closed Won / Closed Lost

### 4. Instant Team Notification

When a lead is fully qualified, your team receives an immediate notification with all the lead details, so you can call back informed and ready.

### 5. Follow-Up Sequence for Non-Replies

Automated follow-up messages for leads who received the first text but did not respond. Three touchpoints over 7 days, then they are tagged and the sequence ends cleanly.
