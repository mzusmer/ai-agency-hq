## What We Will Build

### 1. Missed Call Text-Back Automation

A GoHighLevel workflow that triggers instantly when the business line receives a missed call. The caller receives a text within seconds.

**What the customer receives:**
> "Hey [First Name], sorry we missed your call. This is [Business Name]. What can we help you with today? Reply STOP to opt out."

### 2. AI Lead Qualification via SMS

An AI agent that handles the initial qualification conversation via text. It will collect:

- Customer's full name
- What service or help they are looking for
- Service address or location (if relevant)
- Description of the issue or request
- Urgency level
- Preferred appointment or callback time

### 3. CRM Pipeline in GoHighLevel

A clean pipeline to track every lead from first contact to booked appointment:

- New Lead
- Texted Back
- Qualified
- Appointment Requested
- Appointment Booked
- No Response
- Won / Lost

### 4. Instant Team Notification

When a lead is fully qualified, the team receives an immediate notification with all lead details, so the follow-up call starts informed.

### 5. Follow-Up Sequence for Non-Replies

Automated follow-up messages for leads who received the first text but did not respond. Three touchpoints over 7 days, then they are tagged and the sequence ends cleanly.
