# Pricing Guide - Summit Automation

---

## Standard Pricing

| Item | Price |
|---|---|
| Setup Fee (one time) | $1,000 |
| Monthly Retainer | $500 to $750/month |

---

## What the Setup Fee Covers

The $1,000 setup fee is a one-time charge. It covers:

- GoHighLevel sub-account setup and configuration
- Missed call text-back workflow build
- AI agent prompt configuration and testing
- Roofing-specific custom fields and CRM pipeline
- Tag system setup
- A2P 10DLC registration assistance
- Pre-launch testing (full QA checklist)
- One 45-minute onboarding call with the client
- One 30-minute post-launch review call

---

## What the Monthly Retainer Covers

The monthly retainer keeps the system running and improving. It covers:

- Ongoing workflow support and troubleshooting
- Monthly performance review
- Message optimization (testing different first texts, follow-up sequences)
- Additions to the follow-up sequence if needed
- Priority support via email or text
- Updates if GHL changes how workflows work

**$500/month:** Standard support (recommended for most clients)
**$750/month:** Enhanced support (more active optimization, faster response time, monthly call included)

---

## Pricing Rationale

Do not apologize for the price. Position it in terms of value.

**The math for a roofer:**

- Average residential roofing job: $8,000 to $15,000
- If this system captures even one additional job per month that would have been a missed call, the system pays for itself many times over
- Most clients see a return on the setup fee within their first booked inspection

**How to present this:**

"The setup is $1,000, one time. Then $500 a month going forward. For most roofers, that's less than the profit margin on a single repair job. If this system helps you book one extra job per month that you would have otherwise lost, you're coming out way ahead."

---

## Discounts and Negotiation Policy

- Do not offer unsolicited discounts
- Do not negotiate the setup fee down (it reflects the real work involved)
- If pressed on the retainer, you can offer to start at $500/month and adjust after 60 days based on performance
- Do not offer free trials or month-to-month without a setup fee
- A free demo and free missed call audit are the loss leader, not a reduced price

---

## Invoicing

- Setup fee: invoiced before work begins (collect before touching GHL)
- Monthly retainer: billed on the first of each month via Stripe or manual invoice
- Net 5 payment terms (due within 5 days of invoice)

---

## Payment Methods

- Credit card via Stripe (preferred)
- ACH bank transfer
- Zelle or check (acceptable for first invoice if needed)

---

## What Is NOT Included

- GoHighLevel subscription cost (client pays this directly, typically $97 to $297/month for GHL)
- Twilio or SMS usage fees (pass-through at cost or client pays directly)
- Google Ads or any paid advertising
- Website design or development
- Any service outside of the missed call automation and CRM pipeline

---

## Future Upsells (Do Not Pitch Until First Offer is Proven)

After 60 to 90 days with a client, potential add-on services include:

- Review request automation (ask happy customers for a Google review)
- Reactivation campaign (text old leads who went cold)
- Referral automation
- Google Ads lead tracking integration

These are not in scope for the current offer. Mention them only if the client brings them up.
