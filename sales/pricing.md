# Pricing Guide - Summit Automation

---

## Standard Pricing

| Item | Price |
|---|---|
| Setup Fee (one time) | $1,000 |
| Monthly Retainer | $500 to $750/month |

---

## What the Setup Fee Covers

The $1,000 setup fee is a one-time charge. It covers:

- GoHighLevel sub-account setup and configuration
- Missed call text-back workflow build
- AI agent prompt configuration and testing (tuned to the client's industry)
- Custom fields and CRM pipeline setup
- Tag system setup
- A2P 10DLC registration assistance
- Pre-launch testing (full QA checklist)
- One 45-minute onboarding call
- One 30-minute post-launch review call

---

## What the Monthly Retainer Covers

The monthly retainer keeps the system running and improving:

- Ongoing workflow support and troubleshooting
- Monthly performance review
- Message optimization (testing different first texts, follow-up timing)
- Updates to the follow-up sequence
- Priority support via email or text
- Updates if GHL changes how workflows function

**$500/month:** Standard support (recommended for most clients)
**$750/month:** Enhanced support (more active optimization, faster response time, monthly call included)

---

## Pricing Rationale

Do not apologize for the price. Position it in terms of value.

**The math for any local service business:**

- Average new customer value: varies by industry, but typically $200 to $5,000+
- If this system helps book even one or two additional appointments per month that would have been lost to a missed call, the setup cost is recovered quickly
- The monthly retainer is usually less than what one lost appointment would have been worth

**How to present this:**

> "The setup is $1,000, one time. Then $500 a month. For most local service businesses, that is less than the value of a single new customer. If this system helps you book one extra appointment per month that you would have otherwise lost, you are already coming out ahead."

---

## Discounts and Negotiation Policy

- Do not offer unsolicited discounts
- Do not negotiate the setup fee down (it reflects the real work involved)
- If pressed on the retainer, you can offer to start at $500/month and review after 60 days
- Do not offer free trials or month-to-month without a setup fee
- A free demo and free missed call audit are the offer. Not a reduced price.

---

## Invoicing

- Setup fee: invoiced before work begins
- Monthly retainer: billed on the first of each month via Stripe or invoice
- Net 5 payment terms (due within 5 days of invoice)

---

## Payment Methods

- Credit card via Stripe (preferred)
- ACH bank transfer
- Zelle or check (acceptable for first invoice if needed)

---

## What Is NOT Included

- GoHighLevel subscription cost (client pays GHL directly, typically $97 to $297/month)
- Twilio or SMS usage fees (pass-through at cost or client pays directly)
- Google Ads or any paid advertising
- Website design or development
- Any service outside of the missed call automation and CRM pipeline

---

## Future Upsells (Do Not Pitch Until Core Offer Is Proven)

After 60 to 90 days with a client, potential add-on services:

- Review request automation (ask satisfied customers for a Google review)
- Reactivation campaigns (re-engage past customers who have gone quiet)
- Referral automation
- Appointment reminder sequences

These are not in scope for the current offer. Mention them only if the client brings them up.
