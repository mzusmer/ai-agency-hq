## The Problem We Solve

Every missed call is a homeowner who is probably going to call the next roofer they find. You don't know who called. You can't call them back fast enough. And your voicemail doesn't close deals.

We set up a system that automatically texts every missed caller within seconds. An AI agent then has a real conversation via text, collects their name, address, roofing issue, urgency, and insurance status, and offers to book a free inspection.

By the time you see the notification, the lead is already qualified and ready for a call.
