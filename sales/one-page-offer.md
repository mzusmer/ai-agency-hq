# Summit Automation - One-Page Offer

## Who We Help

Local service businesses that are losing customers because they miss calls. If your team gets inbound calls to book appointments, schedule estimates, or answer service questions, and you can't always answer every one, this is for you.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Every missed call is a customer who probably moves on to the next business they find. You can't call them back fast enough. Your voicemail doesn't book appointments. And most of the time, you don't even know who called.

Most local businesses lose 20 to 40 percent of their inbound calls to no-answer and no-follow-up.

---

## What We Do

We set up a system that automatically texts every missed caller within seconds. An AI agent then has a real conversation via text, collects their name, what they need, their location, how urgent it is, and when they want to be contacted. The business gets notified the moment a qualified lead is ready.

---

## What You Get

- Missed call text-back triggered within seconds of a missed call
- AI-powered SMS qualification (sounds human, not robotic)
- Questions tailored to the type of service the business provides
- Instant notification with full lead details when someone is qualified
- CRM pipeline to track every lead from first contact to booked appointment
- Follow-up sequence for leads who don't respond right away
- Full setup, testing, and support
