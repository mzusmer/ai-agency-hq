## Column Reference

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| Company Name | Full legal or DBA business name |
| Owner Name | First and last name of the decision maker |
| Industry | Type of business (e.g., HVAC, Med Spa, Landscaping) |
| Website | Company website URL |
| Phone | Business phone number (primary line) |
| Email | Owner or business email if found |
| City | City where the business operates |
| State | State (two-letter abbreviation) |
| Google Reviews | Total number of reviews on Google Business Profile |
| Rating | Star rating (e.g., 4.7) |
| Google Business Profile URL | Direct link to their GBP listing |
| Services Offered | What they do |
| Notes | Anything useful from research or conversations |
| Pain Point | Specific pain point identified (missed calls, no follow-up, after-hours gaps, etc.) |
| Outreach Status | Current stage in outreach (see status list below) |
| Last Contacted | Date of most recent contact (YYYY-MM-DD) |
| Next Follow-Up Date | When to follow up next (YYYY-MM-DD) |
| Booked Demo | Yes / No |
| Closed | Yes / No |
