# Daily Activity Tracker - Summit Automation

Use this every day to stay consistent and track your progress toward landing and serving clients.

---

## How to Use This Tracker

Fill out the table at the end of each day. Be honest. The goal is to see patterns: which activities produce results, where time is being wasted, and whether you are on pace to hit your goals.

---

## Daily Minimum Activity Targets (Pre-Client Phase)

| Activity | Daily Target |
|---|---|
| New businesses researched | 10 |
| Cold emails sent | 5 |
| Instagram DMs sent | 10 |
| Cold calls made | 10 |
| Loom videos recorded and sent | 1 to 2 |
| Follow-ups sent to previous leads | All who are due |

---

## Weekly Goal (Pre-Client Phase)

- 1 demo booked per week minimum
- 1 proposal sent per active demo
- Progress toward first client sign

---

## Daily Check-In Questions

Answer these each morning before outreach:

1. What is the one thing I need to accomplish today to move closer to a signed client?
2. Who on my lead list is due for follow-up today?
3. Is there any blocker I need to clear before starting outreach?

---

## End-of-Day Review

Answer these at the end of each day:

1. Did I hit my outreach targets?
2. What worked today (got a reply, booked a call, had a good conversation)?
3. What did not work?
4. What do I need to do first thing tomorrow?

---

## Tracker Table

Use the data tracker table at `data/daily-tracker.md` to log each day's numbers.

---

## Notes

- Consistency beats intensity. 10 outreach attempts per day for 30 days is 300 touches. That gets results.
- Log everything, even the days with zero results. It shows you where the dry streaks happen.
- After each call or conversation, log notes in `data/lead-list.md` under the Notes column.
- Review the week every Friday and adjust your outreach approach if something is not working after 5 to 7 days.
