# Lead List Template Guide - Summit Automation

This guide explains how to build and use the prospect list for outreach to local service businesses.

---

## Overview

The lead list is where you track every local business you are considering reaching out to, are actively contacting, or have already spoken with. It lives in `data/lead-list.md`.

---

## How to Find Local Service Businesses to Contact

### Google Maps
1. Open Google Maps
2. Search: "[service type] [city, state]" (e.g., "HVAC company Dallas TX" or "med spa Austin TX")
3. Click each listing and capture relevant info from the profile and their website

### Google Business Profile Search
1. Search: "[service type] near [city]" in Google Search
2. The local pack shows top 3 results
3. Click "More places" to see additional listings

### Instagram
1. Search hashtags: #[cityname][servicetype] (e.g., #dallasmassage, #denverhvac)
2. Look for business accounts posting service work, results, or before/afters
3. Cross-reference with Google to get contact details

### Industry Directories
- Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack for home services
- Zocdoc for health/wellness businesses
- Yelp for any local business category

## What to Look For in a Good Prospect

Strong fits:
- Active Google Business Profile (recent reviews, updated photos)
- 15 to 300 reviews (established enough to have call volume, small enough to not have a full call center)
- 4.0 star rating or higher (healthy business with real customers)
- Phone number listed as the primary contact method
- Owner-operated or small team (they feel the pain of missed calls directly)

Weak fits:
- Large franchises or corporate chains (they have systems)
- Brand new businesses with 0 to 5 reviews (not enough call volume yet)
- Businesses with no Google presence and no website

## Outreach Status Values

Use these consistently in the Status column:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Not Started | Found but haven't reached out yet |
| Email Sent | First cold email sent |
| DM Sent | Social DM sent |
| Called | Cold call made (answer or voicemail) |
| Loom Sent | Personalized Loom video delivered |
| Replied | They responded to any outreach |
| Demo Booked | Demo call is scheduled |
| Demo Completed | Demo happened |
| Proposal Sent | Written proposal delivered |
| Negotiating | In back-and-forth on pricing or terms |
| Closed Won | Signed and paid |
| Closed Lost | Passed or not a fit |
| Not Interested | Explicitly declined |
| Follow Up Later | Timing is not right, try again in X weeks |

---

## Data Storage

The actual tracking table is in `data/lead-list.md`. Add a new row for each business you research or contact. Update the row when the status changes or new information is learned.

---

## Privacy Note

This list contains business contact information sourced from public sources (Google, websites, social media). Do not upload it to any public repository.
