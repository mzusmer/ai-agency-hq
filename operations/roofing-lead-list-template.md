# Roofing Lead List Template Guide - Summit Automation

This guide explains how to build and use the prospect list for outreach.

---

## Overview

The roofing lead list is where you track every roofing company you are considering reaching out to, are actively reaching out to, or have already contacted. It lives in `data/roofing-lead-list.md`.

---

## How to Find Roofing Companies to Contact

### Google Maps
1. Open Google Maps
2. Search: "roofing company [city, state]"
3. Look through the results
4. Click each listing and capture the relevant information from the listing and their website

### Google Business Profile Search
1. Search: "roofing contractor [city]" in Google Search
2. The local pack (map results) shows the top 3 listings
3. Click "More places" to see additional results
4. Visit each profile for contact details and review data

### Instagram
1. Search hashtags: #roofingcompany, #[city]roofer, #roofingcontractor
2. Search business accounts: look for accounts posting job site photos
3. Find the business name and cross-reference with Google

### Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack
1. Search for roofing companies in a target city
2. These platforms list company names, service areas, and sometimes contact info

## What to Look For in a Good Prospect

Strong fits:
- Active Google Business Profile (recent reviews, photos)
- 10 to 200 reviews (established enough to have call volume, small enough to not have a full office staff)
- 4.0 star rating or higher (healthy business with real customers)
- Phone number listed on GBP (relies on inbound calls)
- Located in storm-prone markets

Weak fits:
- Very large companies (5+ trucks, corporate look) - they probably have systems
- Brand new businesses with 0 to 5 reviews - not enough call volume yet
- Companies with no online presence - harder to reach

## Outreach Status Values

Use these consistently in the Status column:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Not Started | Found but haven't reached out yet |
| Email Sent | First cold email sent |
| DM Sent | Instagram or social DM sent |
| Called | Cold call made (answer or voicemail) |
| Loom Sent | Personalized Loom video delivered |
| Replied | They responded to any outreach |
| Demo Booked | Demo call is scheduled |
| Demo Completed | Demo happened |
| Proposal Sent | Written proposal delivered |
| Negotiating | In back-and-forth on pricing or terms |
| Closed Won | Signed and paid |
| Closed Lost | Passed or not a fit |
| Not Interested | Explicitly declined |
| Follow Up Later | Said timing is not right, try again in X weeks |

---

## Data Storage

The actual tracking table is in `data/roofing-lead-list.md`. Add a new row for each company you research or contact. Update the row whenever the status changes or new information is learned.

## Privacy Note

This list contains business contact information sourced from public sources (Google, websites, social media). Do not share this file or upload it to any public repository.
