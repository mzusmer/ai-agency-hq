Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Summit Automation ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or submit a form to request our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: Name, company name, phone number, email address, and state.
  • Business information: Team size, current lead challenges, and other details you provide in our contact form.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, and IP address collected automatically via standard web analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry and schedule your free demo or audit
  • To send you relevant information about our services via email or phone
  • To send you SMS messages about your inquiry if you have provided your phone number and consented to receive texts
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. SMS Communications

By submitting a form on our website and providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Summit Automation related to your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

  • To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us.
  • To get help, reply HELP.
  • We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party platforms (such as GoHighLevel and email tools) that help us operate our business. These providers are contractually required to protect your information.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
